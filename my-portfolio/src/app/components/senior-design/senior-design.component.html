Senior Design

Website

Definition

Our Senior Design project focuses on keeping elderly seniors inside their home. To do this, we will implement a sensor network and utilize a wearable (such as a smart watch) to track the senior's activity, and identify when the senior does an activity (such as make breakfast). When an activity is identified, this will be logged to develop a character profile of the senior. That way, eventually if the senior does not follow along their regular schedule, their caretakers and family members will be notified. Our project will inherit previous design projects. As a result, the scope of our project has been limited to three things: Implementing a smart-plug tracking system, a on-senior device wearable tracking system, and a guest tracking system. These will then need to be integrated into current back-end environment. Our hope is that our project can help seniors stay in their homes longer, and also alert the senior's caretakers and family member's if something goes wrong. Our hope is that by tracking if the senior is following their regular schedule, we can alert caretakers before something goes wrong.
